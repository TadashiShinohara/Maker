The Decision Making Tool for Capital Investments

What is Maker?

Maker leads you to good capital investments.

There are big risks of introducing new capital investments especially during economic uncertainty. Not sure about what happens tomorrow. There's no crystal ball to tell about the future. But your instinct is telling you it's the right moment to take a chance? Then, you can measure the risks based on objective value evaluations.

Maker helps a decision making of new capital investments. It will calculate the economic value and will tell you whether you should go for it.

Tools

Maker is composed of three parts, Assumptions, Calculation, and Decision Maker. First, Assumptions are key factor inputs such as Cost of Capital, Investment Term, Cash Flows etc. Second, Calculation will show you economic values from your investment by three indicators, Net Present Value(NPV), Profitability Index(PI), and Internal Rate of Return(IRR). Finally, Decision Maker will suggest a go or no-go result about your investment.
