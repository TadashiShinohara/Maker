The Decision Making Tool for Capital Investments

What is Maker?

Maker leads you to good capital investments.

There are big risks to introduce new capital investments especially in times of economic uncertainty. Not sure about what happens tomorrow. There's no crystal ball to tell about the future. But it doesn't mean we shouldn't try. Based on objective evaluations, we can measure the risks to some extent.

Maker helps a decision making of new capital investments. It calculates the economic value and shows you whether or not you should implement ones.

Tools

Maker comprises three parts, Assumptions, Calculation, and Decision Maker. First, Assumptions are a key factor input area such as Cost of Capital, Investment Term, Cash Flows etc. Second, Calculation shows you economic values from the investment in three ways, Net Present Value, Profitability Index, and Internal Rate of Return. Finally, Decision Maker suggests a go or no-go about the investment.
